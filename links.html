For further information on Bataille and his work, please consult the following brief bibliography.

  • This LibGuide from Libarian Michael Kicey at the University of Buffalo contains a plethora of information on Bataille and his work, including a list of his work in both French and translation, criticism of Bataille's work in English, a list of artists and writers associated with Bataille, and more.

  • Monoskop has information on and PDFs of both Acéphale and Documents.

  • Gallica has a number of resources on Bataille, including PDFs of Documents.

  • Finally, the Wikipedia article for Bataille contains introductory biographical information that will be useful for those unfamiliar with his life and work.
